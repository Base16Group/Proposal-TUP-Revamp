Courses

The Technological University of the Philippines offers comprehensive programs that blend theory and practice to develop skilled, innovative professionals prepared for future careers.

College of Industrial Education

Trains future educators with both teaching skills and technical expertise. Graduates are equipped to teach and manage programs in technology and vocational education.

College of Engineering

Develops problem-solving and design skills through rigorous engineering education. Students are trained to create sustainable and innovative solutions for real-world challenges.

College of Liberal Arts

Promotes critical thinking, communication, and cultural awareness through studies in the humanities and social sciences. It prepares students for careers in education, media, and public service.

College of Science

Focuses on scientific inquiry and research across natural and applied sciences. Students gain knowledge and skills for innovation in scientific and technological fields.

College of Architecture and Fine Arts

Combines creativity and technical proficiency in the fields of architecture, design, and the visual arts. Students learn to design functional and inspiring spaces and artworks that enhance communities.
